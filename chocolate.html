Xocolātl

From Náhuatl, with love

Although the exact etymology of the word is debated, it comes originally from Nahuatl, the language of the Aztec culture.

Some propose it is derived from chicolatl, "beaten drink". Others say it comes from chocóatl, "bitter water".

What is definitely true is that many languages have adopted the word with small changes in its writing and pronunciation.
